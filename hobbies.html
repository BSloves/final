My Hobbies

Disc Golf

Disc golf is a relatively new hobby of mine, spanning about 2 years now. However, I am not new to frisbee having grown up playing a ton of frisbee with my friends everyday every summer. I had played a few times a year for a while, but during my co-op rotation in fall of 2024 my co-workers got me really into it. It is now a casual hobby that I like to participate in when the weather is nice.

Cooking

Throughout the first 18 years of my life, and still to this day when I am home, my dad has cooked family dinner every night. After moving away to college it became my responsiblity to actually feed myself. After a year of living off of the dining hall food, I decided to learn how to cook just as my dad did in college.

3D Modeling/Printing

3D modeling has been a fun hobby of mine ever since I first learned Fusion360 in 2017. It has allowed me to design and print anything from fun little knick knacks to more useful items like bag clips and load bearing L-brackets.

Lego

As an engineer I feel as if an interest in Lego is a right of passage. It is definitely an expensive passage. Especially as a Star Wars fan. A few of my favorite, and also largest, sets that I have acquired recently are the UCS Millennium Falcon, the UCS R2D2, the Spider-Man Frame, and the Globe.
